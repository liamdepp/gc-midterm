Once upon a time in the mystical land of Downtown Detroit there dwelt an immortal named Kai-Yun and his trusty band of ninja students.

They were very clever students indeed, known for their ingenious teamwork.

The students trained their entire lives for whatever mission they would be sent on.

Their master David sent them out to defeat the evil Professor David.

On their way to clash with Professor David, the students ran into a beggar, Mitch.

The monekeys kindly gave Mitch some money and advised him spend it on a coding bootcamp

However. Mitch did not spend the money on a coding bootcamp and gave the money to Professor David to fund his army of robots.

With his evil army of coding robots, David trapped the students in an infinite loop. The end.
