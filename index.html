Once upon a time in the mystical land of Shangrila there dwelt an immortal named Kai-Yun and his trusty band of ninja monkeys.

They were very clever monkeys indeed, known for their ingenious teamwork.

The monkeys trained their entire lives for whatever mission they would be sent on.

Their master Kai-Yun sent them out to defeat the great Professor David.

On their way to clash with David, the monkeys ran into a beggar, Mitch.
